About

Looking for more information about the event? Look no further!

You're invited to Stanford's Class of 2023 Senior Formal!! Come spend an unforgettable night with your classmates at the Exploratorium in San Francisco, on April 14th from 9 PM - 1 AM. There will be food, drinks, photo booths, DJ ASAP, and lots of exhibits to explore. Be sure to purchase a ticket through our Eventbrite to reserve your spot. We can't wait to see you at the Exploratorium on April 14!

Ticket Information

  • Opportunity-Fund Eligible: $50
  • Early-Bird Pricing: $65, 300 tickets available
  • General Admission: $75, 1000 tickets available
  • Late: $100, 500 tickets available
  • Bus Tickets: $25, Needed to ride the busses to and from the Exploratorium

**Students eligible for the Opportunity Fund will have already received a discount code from the financial aid office, which can be applied to the General Admission ticket for a $25 discount. The General Admission ticket is also open so you can buy it now! Also, bus tickets are not covered by the discount code.**
