Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about the event? Look through some of the frequently asked questions or reach out!

  • What is the guest policy?

    You are welcome to bring a +1 to Senior Formal! They will have to purchase a ticket through the Eventbrite as well.

  • What will the transportation situation be?

    There will be buses going to and from the Exploratorium which can be accessed through purchasing a bus ticket. Buses will be leaving from Tresidder at 8 PM and returning from the Exploratorium at 1 AM. There are 1200 bus tickets available!

  • What should I bring?

    Be sure to bring your government ID (to check if you are 21+) and your Eventbrite ticket.

  • What should I not bring?

    Do not bring any outside alcohol, substances, or devices; they will be confiscated at the door.
