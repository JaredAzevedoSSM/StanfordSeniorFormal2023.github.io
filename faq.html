Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about the event? Look through some of the frequently asked questions or reach out!

  • Discounted hotel rooms?

    Attendees will have the option to purchase a discounted hotel room for the night ($139) at the Harbor Court Hotel, a stylish hotel by the waterfront that is a walking distance from the Exploratorium. There are still hotel rooms available to be booked at a discounted rate here. Check out this list of fun activities for spending the weekend in SF!

  • What is the guest policy?

    You are welcome to bring a +1 to Senior Formal! They will have to purchase a ticket through the Eventbrite as well.

  • What should I bring?

    Be sure to bring your government ID (to check if you are 21+) and your Eventbrite ticket.

  • What should I not bring?

    Do not bring any outside alcohol, substances, or vapes; they will be confiscated at the door.

  • What is the dress code?

    This is a formal event, so dress to impress!

  • Are dates expected?

    Dates are not expected! Feel free to ask a date to go with you, but it is by no means necessary or encouraged.

  • What will the transportation situation be?

    There will be buses going to and from the Exploratorium which can be accessed by purchasing a bus ticket. Buses will be leaving from Tresidder at 8 PM and returning from the Exploratorium at 1 AM. There are 1200 bus tickets available and they must be purchased by 4/6 at 5 PM.

  • Why are the tickets so expensive?

    Senior Formal is (and has historically been) self-sustainable, so the expenses of the event are covered entirely by ticket sales and sponsorships. General admission ticket prices have varied in the past years from $65 to $140, depending on event costs and sponsorship received. For reference, the 2018 Senior Formal had a $90 general admission ticket and a $35 bus ticket.
    Event-related costs have also significantly increased compared to previous years due to inflation and other economic factors. Additionally, there has been less availability of University sponsorship this year, especially compared to last year.
    We have truly made every effort to reduce the price of tickets, and these prices are the best we can do. We appreciate your patience and understanding, and we hope you enjoy the event!
