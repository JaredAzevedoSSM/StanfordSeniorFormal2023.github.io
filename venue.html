The Exploratorium, San Francisco

We are very excited to host Senior Formal at the Exploratorium on Pier 15 in San Francisco! Come explore the Osher West Gallery, the Bechtel Central Gallery, & Outdoor Gallery and get ready to make many memories. Each of these galleries have themed interactive exhibits that students can explore. The Osher West Gallery integrates exhibits on psychology and art, while the Bechtel Central Gallery explores sights, sounds, and perception, so come ready for a great time!
