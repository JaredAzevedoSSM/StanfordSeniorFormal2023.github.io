Rules & Security

In order to ensure a fun and safe night, the Exploratorium has created certain rules and security measures that students must adhere to in order to attend.

The Rules

  1. You will need to bring your government ID to be allowed in (or passport for international students).
  2. Intoxicated students may be denied entry.
  3. Full pat-downs of students will take place.
  4. All bags will be searched at entry for alcohol and substances.
  5. No Ins & Outs; If you leave you will not be allowed back in.
  6. Vaping is not permitted indoors, and will be confiscated.
  7. The Exploratorium has a zero substance tolerance policy, if anyone is found using a controlled substance they will be removed immediately.
  8. The West Gallery will be used as the 21+ Biergarten. Alcohol will not be allowed to leave the area, you will need to finish your drink in the Biergarten.

If you are denied entry or kicked out of the venue, you will be financially responsible for getting yourself back to campus. Senior Leadership cannot help financially.
