Rules & Security

In order to ensure a fun and safe night, the Exploratorium has created certain rules and security measures that are important to be aware of.

The Rules

  1. You will need to bring your government ID to be allowed in (or passport for international students).
  2. Visibly intoxicated students will be denied entry.
  3. Students will be pat down at entry (checking for alcohol and prohibited devices)
  4. All bags will be searched at entry for alcohol and substances.
  5. No Ins & Outs; If you leave you will not be allowed back in.
  6. Do not bring vapes into the venue. Vaping is not permitted indoors, and will be confiscated by security. The fire alarms are also very sensitive and triggering them will require everyone to evacuate the venue.
  7. The Exploratorium has a zero substance tolerance policy, if anyone is found using a controlled substance they will be removed immediately.
  8. The Central Gallery will be restricted to guests who are 21+ because that is where alcohol will be served. You are not allowed to bring alcohol outside of the Central Gallery.

If you are denied entry or kicked out of the venue, you will be financially responsible for getting yourself back to campus.
